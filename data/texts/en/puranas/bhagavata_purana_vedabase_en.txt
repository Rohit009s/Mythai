SRIMAD BHAGAVATAM - CANTO 1
Creation
============================================================


CHAPTER 1
----------------------------------------

Verse 1: O my Lord, the all-pervading Personality of Godhead, I offer my respectful obeisances unto You.

Verse 2: O Lord, You are the supreme controller, the Personality of Godhead, the supreme great, maintaining the order of this cosmic manifestation.

Verse 3: By Your mercy only can one understand the transcendental nature of Your holy name, form, qualities, and pastimes.
